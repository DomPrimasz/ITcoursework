About Us

TangleWood College is a place of passion and learning. We have employed over 200 knowledgeable staff members in our place of learning and we reside well established in our local area. We are greatly involved in community projects and events in our area, and we succeeded in raising a total Â£50,000 for numerous charities, including: WaterAid, Christian Aid, British Red Cross, and many more. All of our students and staff help to build a friendly environment to welcome everyone into our campus.

The college was first established in 1991 aiming to provide an immaculate learning environment where every student would have the opportunity to become something more. Over the years, the college has expanded in every fields and has developed its course range and number of helpful staff by a tremendous amount.

Thanks to its efforts, it has seen great success in seeking every one of its students to their next step in education with a head start that no other institution could provide.

Why choose us?

We offer a great range of valued and loved courses. In fact, out of 90% of the colleges in the UK, we offer the most amount of varied courses, so whatever any student may love doing they can be sure to find something to suit their taste here!

We have won numerous awards from different awards organisations, praising us for our high-quality education and peaceful environments. Our staff members are all supportive members of a collaborative effort to pass on knowledge to aspiring students. We have only hired the best of the best, english teachers ranging from university professors to book authors, music teachers ranging from band members to pop artists, we at Tanglewood want to make sure that our students get the best possible education that they can!

When students first come to our college, it may be a bit overwhelming at first to get used to the layout and where everything is, and so we want to make sure we support all of our students throughout their entire journey with us, onto their next step in education.

What we provide:

Students who sign up with us can be sure they will receive the education and support they desire. All students who come to our college are automatically signed up for a prospects programme, aimed at preparing students for their next step be it university or perhaps a job career, we want to make sure that students get the most out of their time here.

We also provide our students with many learning resources that they can access either on-site or at home, to further consolidate their learning. This is mostly achieved by an intranet service our students are given access to, this service allows students to view their status reports, access learning resources and reach out to a support team or any of their teachers.

Whatever the situation may be, we will gladly support our students throughout their whole journey with us to make sure they will leave well-prepared.
