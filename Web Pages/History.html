Learn History with Us

Take this one-time opportunity to sign up for our state-of-the-art History course, where we take a deep dwell into the past
deeds of humanity ranging from The Second World War all the way back to the Cambrian Explosion.
Whatever it is you may be interested in, it will be here in the course, where we will take a deep dive head first into the content



This course will feature a lot of essay based knowledge, where students will be tested on their knowledge and understanding,
well as their interpretation skills. Our classes will aim to engage our learners in group activities that are meant to help
develop students' social skills and strengthen their abilities to work together in a team, as working together is a crucial part of our
image as the college.

Entry Requirements

A minimum of five (5) grade 4 GCSEs with a grade 4 in English and mathematics being a requirement. Any entries that do not meet the English and mathematics requirements will be considered on a case-by-case basis. Any candidates accepted without the sufficient grades in English and mathematics will be required to take these subjects separately, where they will be prepared to take the examinations again to get the desired grades.

If any questions arise, contact us on the enquiry web page.
